Meet your Trainer.

Hi! I’m Shekar, a fitness science enthusiast, self-made athlete, and health coach. I’ve trained many people across India to tone up, get lean abs, lose fat, and build a body they feel proud of.

I started my fitness journey at 17, and it’s been a rollercoaster ride. I’ve gone from gaining weight to losing it, trying endless experiments and training to reach a sustainable, fit, and healthy body. I’ve also experimented with body recomposition and lean bulking.

I’m the person you’ll be proud of—I attempted the world’s toughest person record: 4100 pull-ups, as set by David Goggins.

I’ve gone through the pain, and I know what it’s like to do this alone. But I promise, you are not alone on this road. I’m here to help you every step of the way.
